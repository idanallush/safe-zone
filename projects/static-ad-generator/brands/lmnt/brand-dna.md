# BRAND DNA DOCUMENT
# Milega (מילגה) — Toscana Sofa Collection

==================

## BRAND OVERVIEW

**Name:** Milega (מילגה)
**Tagline:** "כל מוצר שלנו מספר סיפור של נוחות ישראלית" (Every product tells a story of Israeli comfort)
**Parent Company:** Wissmann Holdings Group (also operates Shomrat HaZore'a / שמרת הזורע)
**Founded:** ~30 years ago by Yogi and Michal Avisher, factory in Or Akiva, Israel
**Design Agency:** In-house (Wissmann Group design team / Betanet Shomrat platform)

**Voice Adjectives [5]:**
1. Warm — familial, emotionally connected, inclusive ("אנחנו")
2. Contemporary — modern design language, current trends
3. Approachable — accessible pricing, casual tone, comfort-first
4. Quality-conscious — premium materials, Israeli manufacturing pride
5. Playful — color variety, mix & match, customization as self-expression

**Positioning:** Israel's leading comfort-lifestyle brand. Bean bags are the heritage, but modular sofas (Toscana, Soho, Marrakesh) are the growth engine. Positioned as affordable premium — quality corduroy, modular flexibility, design-forward but not intimidating. "Good design improves quality of life, adds color to routine, and brings daily joy."

**Competitive Differentiation:**
- vs. Shomrat HaZore'a (sister brand): Milega is younger, more playful, color-driven. Shomrat is traditional furniture retail.
- vs. Leonardo Furniture (lnrd.co.il): Leonardo sells formal living room sets with recliners. Milega's Toscana is modular, casual, corduroy-based — younger audience.
- vs. Mia Furniture (miafurniture.co.il): Mia offers classic Tuscany-style sofas. Milega's Toscana is modern, modular, and customizable with mix & match colors.
- vs. Dov Dvan (dovdvan.com): Traditional modular sofas. Milega differentiates with fabric texture (corduroy), bold color options, and playful branding.

## PHOTOGRAPHY DIRECTION

**Lighting:** Soft, diffused studio lighting. Even illumination across the product. No harsh shadows. Warm color temperature leaning slightly toward golden/amber.
**Color Grading:** Neutral-warm. True-to-product color representation with a slight warm lift. Clean whites, no heavy filters or moody grading.
**Composition:** Product-centered, shot from a slight 3/4 angle or front-facing. Clean negative space around the sofa. Some lifestyle shots show the sofa in a styled living room context with minimal props.
**Subject Matter:** The sofa itself is always the hero. Lifestyle images show bright, airy Israeli living rooms with natural light. Occasional human interaction (sitting, lounging) but product-focused.
**Props and Surfaces:** Light wood floors, white/cream walls, minimal decorative pillows, a throw blanket, maybe a side table with a plant. Nothing competing with the sofa for attention.
**Mood:** Inviting, calm, lived-in but curated. "Come sit down" energy. Comfortable but stylish.

## PRODUCT DETAILS — Toscana Sofa

**Physical Description:** Modular corduroy sofa with clean, low-profile contemporary lines. Boxy-soft silhouette — structured enough to look designed, soft enough to look comfortable. Deep seat, generous proportions. Ribbed corduroy texture is visually distinctive and tactile.

**Configurations:**
- Single armchair (כורסת יחיד) — ₪1,490
- Double sofa (ספה זוגית) — ₪2,850
- Triple sofa (ספה שלישייה) — ₪3,900
- Four-seat sofa (ספה 4 יחידות) — ₪4,950
- Mix & Match double (שילובי צבעים) — ₪2,850

**Distinctive Features:**
- Corduroy fabric — soft, luxurious, ribbed texture (בד קורדרוי רך ומפנק)
- Quality durable foam (ספוג איכותי ועמיד)
- Full modularity — units connect and rearrange
- Mix & Match option — combine two different corduroy colors in one sofa
- 5 color options
- Free shipping (משלוח חינם)
- Fast delivery (אספקה מהירה)

**Label-Logo Placement:** Milega logo on the sofa is subtle — typically a small tag on the lower back or underside. The brand presence is through the packaging and marketing, not loud on-product branding.

**Packaging System:** Delivered in branded Milega packaging. Modular units ship individually, allowing flexible delivery.

## AD CREATIVE STYLE

**Typical formats:** Product-hero shots on clean backgrounds (white/off-white). Lifestyle-in-context showing the sofa in a bright living room. Color swatch arrays showing all 5 options. Configuration diagrams showing modularity.
**Text overlay style:** Bold Hebrew sans-serif headlines. Clean, minimal. White or dark text depending on background. Badges for "חדש!" (New!), "משלוח חינם" (Free shipping), "רב מכר" (Bestseller).
**Photo vs illustration:** 100% photography. No illustrations. Real product, real settings.
**UGC usage:** Minimal currently — brand-produced content dominates. Opportunity for UGC-style ads showing real homes.
**Offer presentation:** Price displayed prominently in ₪. Free shipping and fast delivery as recurring value props. Bundle pricing for larger configurations.
